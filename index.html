Hi, I'm a developer that loves clean & elegant code.

A little bit about me.

My home base is Shreveport, Louisiana, with my family. My journey into tech started years ago when a friend and I started messing around with a Commodore 128. That was a long time ago! I was involved in ministry at that time, but was caught up in computer tech, games, and communications. As internet technology grew through the years, I stayed connected with what was happening in the tech world. My wife began a home-based soap business, and I decided that my part in helping her would be to get her business connected to the web. I was intrigued by the world of web development and the power of internet technology.

That curiosity in exploring new tech skills opened a door that I knew could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom and versatility I was looking for in my work. I could not only help our little soaping business, but I could help many, many others. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer's PSD and quickly and accurately convert it into a webpage that is pixel-perfect match.
